Double Room in spacious house (short-term)

Click to zoom

This is a fixed-term let from mid April until the 15th of July, 2022. Terms are negotiable (excluding the end date).

Lived here for almost 6 years. Love this place. Landlord is great, but is now giving the property to his kids. That's the only reason I'm leaving.

The house is a 3 minute walk from Southfields station (District Line). The 39 Bus goes right by the house and is a direct link to Clapham Junction. Good selection of shops (e. g. M&S Foothill, Sainsbury Local, Tesco Express, Boots, Cook, Cafe Nero, Gail's, etc.). Southside Shopping Centre is a 15 minute walk away. Wimbledon Park is close by, as well as Richmond Park.

The current housemates are all professionals and are looking for someone friendly and considerate to live with. While everyone has their own lives, housemates do like to hang out from time to time.

The room is a very large double on the ground floor. It comes with a bed, desk, wardrobe, chest of drawers, bedside table and lamp.

The house is modern and spacious. Open-plan lounge and dining room. Kitchen is large, with all the modern utilities (e. g. dishwasher), and lots of space. Separate utility room outside for washer and dryer. Shed in the back of the garden for storage.

There is a main bathroom upstairs with a shower over the bath, another separate shower room upstairs, and a downstairs toilet.

The house has a garden, with decking, a picnic table and outdoor lighting, and a BBQ. There is a garden shed with ch can be used for storage (e. g. a bike).

Additional storage can be found in the attic. There is also a small spare room which can be used for guests or as extra storage space.

Bills currently cost about £100 per month which includes: council tax, water, gas, electricity, broadband internet/WiFi, Virgin Media, TV licence and Netflix.
